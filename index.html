All Jackson County Democrats & interested citizens are invited to join us!

Special Guest: Jeff Berrong

Jeff Berrong will discuss State Question 836, which would replace Oklahoma’s closed primary with a “Top Two” Open Primary system.

A resident of Weatherford, Berrong is a third-generation owner in his family’s independent insurance agency, Ed Berrong Insurance Agency, Inc., . He received a Bachelor of Business Administration degree from the University of Oklahoma in 2004. Before returning home to western Oklahoma in 2006, Jeff served as a field representative for former Oklahoma Congressman Dan Boren. He is very involved in his community where he has served on the board of directors of the Great Plains Family YMCA, the Weatherford Rotary Club, the Weatherford Area Chamber of Commerce, and the Weatherford Industrial Trust. Jeff has enjoyed a lifelong interest in public policy, government, and politics and was a candidate for the Oklahoma State Senate District 38 seat in 2018.

Lately you can often find Jeff on KFOR's Flash Point, where he discusses Democratic Party topics and issues concerning Oklahoma in general. Watch Jeff Berrong on a recent episode of KFOR's Flash Point, where he and former Lt. Governor Todd Lamb discuss the topic of SQ 836.
